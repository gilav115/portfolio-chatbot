# Services

**Fractional CPO**: Embedded product leadership for companies that need senior ownership without a full-time hire. Works alongside the CEO and engineering lead to define roadmap, run discovery, and build product culture.

**Product Strategy**: Structured discovery, roadmap definition, prioritisation frameworks, and alignment across engineering, design, and sales. Particularly useful at inflection points: post-funding, pre-launch, or when existing strategy has stalled.

**Team Coaching**: Working with product managers individually or as a team to improve discovery habits, stakeholder communication, and delivery velocity.

**Product Audit**: A structured review of current roadmap, process, and team setup, with a written report and prioritised recommendations. Typical engagement is two to three weeks.
