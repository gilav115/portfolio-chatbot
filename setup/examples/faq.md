# FAQ

**Do you work with early-stage startups?**
Yes, though the work is most effective post-PMF when there is enough of a product to structure around. Pre-PMF engagements tend to focus on discovery process and prioritisation rather than team structure.

**What does a fractional CPO engagement look like?**
Typically two or three days per week embedded with the team over a three to six month period. Involvement includes roadmap planning, weekly leadership sync, design and engineering collaboration, and stakeholder communication.

**Do you work remotely?**
Primarily remote. On-site in London or travel for important workshops or team events when it genuinely adds value.

**Can you help if we already have a product team?**
Yes. Coaching and strategy work is designed to complement an existing team, not replace it.

**How quickly can you start?**
Usually within two to three weeks depending on current commitments. Book an intro call to check current availability.

**What is the typical engagement length?**
Product audits run two to three weeks. Fractional and strategy work typically runs three to six months with a monthly rolling arrangement after the initial period.
